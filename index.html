About Us

Golden Garden is split into Golden Garden Sand and Gravel and Golden Garden Landscaping products. Our company was founded in 2007 and has grown a lot in landscaping products.

Our company has been in business since 2007. During the past few years our business has grown in landscaping products.

We started by selling our own beech pebble in sizes of 6mm to 20mm and expanded in sourcing stone from all over Ireland, which today gives us one of the most extensive range of products for the Garden & Landscaping business.

We supply most of our products in bags from 25kg up to 1ton.
